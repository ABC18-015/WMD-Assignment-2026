Our Story

Rooted in Botswana, committed to authentic safari experiences

Founded in Botswana, For the World

Mission

To share Botswana's extraordinary wilderness and rich cultural heritage through ethical, sustainable, and transformative safari experiences that benefit local communities and protect wildlife for future generations.

Vision

To be Southern Africa's most trusted safari operator, recognized for authentic experiences, conservation leadership, and meaningful community partnerships.

History

Mosepele was founded in 2015 by Bobby Kokome, a Maun-born safari guide with deep roots in Botswana's tourism industry. What began as small walking safaris has grown into a full-service safari company offering experiences across all of Botswana's iconic wilderness areas.

"Mosepele means 'journey' in Setswana. We chose this name because every safari is more than a trip - it's a transformative journey through one of Africa's last great wilderness areas."

— Bobby Kokome, Founder

Our Sustainability Commitment

Tourism done right protects wilderness, empowers communities, and creates lasting positive impact. We're committed to operating in ways that ensure Botswana's wildlife and culture thrive for generations.

  • All vehicles run on low-emission engines; carbon offset for every safari
  • Zero single-use plastic policy across all camps and tours
  • 10% of profits support Botswana Wildlife Conservation Trust
  • Employ only local guides and staff from neighboring communities
  • Solar power at all permanent camps
  • Water conservation and greywater recycling systems
