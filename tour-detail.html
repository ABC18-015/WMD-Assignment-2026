Tour Overview

Experience the magic of the Okavango Delta, one of Africa's last great wilderness areas. This 5-day safari takes you deep into the heart of the world's largest inland delta, where crystal-clear waterways create a lush oasis teeming with wildlife.

Navigate pristine channels by traditional mokoro canoe, embark on thrilling game drives, and spend nights under a blanket of stars at our exclusive mobile camp. Led by expert local guides born and raised in the Delta, this journey offers an authentic and immersive safari experience.

Highlights

  • Mokoro canoe excursions
  • Morning & afternoon game drives
  • Walking safaris with armed guides
  • Bird watching (over 400 species)
  • Sunset boat cruise
  • Visit to local village

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Maun

Arrive at Maun Airport. Meet your guide and transfer to our camp on the edge of the Delta. Evening orientation and welcome dinner under the stars.

Day 2: Mokoro Canoe Adventure

Morning mokoro excursion through narrow channels. Afternoon game drive in search of elephants, lions, and giraffes. Night safari drive after dinner.

Day 3: Walking Safari & Birding

Guided walking safari to track wildlife on foot. Afternoon bird watching excursion. Visit to a local village to experience traditional life.

Day 4: Full Day Game Drive

Full day game drive with picnic lunch. Explore remote areas of the Delta. Sunset cruise on the lagoon with sundowners.

Day 5: Departure

Morning game drive followed by breakfast. Transfer to Maun Airport for your onward journey.
